# Shahjalal Enterprise Realistic Banner Prompts (16:9 Aspect Ratio)

This document contains **3 highly realistic, professional commercial photography prompts** tailored specifically for M/S. Shah Jalal Enterprise's global sourcing, import/export, and medical tourism consultancy services.

To ensure compatibility with left-aligned text overlays and buttons, these prompts are structured for an **asymmetrical composition (rule of thirds)**, placing the primary subject on the right and leaving clean copy space on the left with a natural shallow depth of field (bokeh).

---

### Banner 1: Global Trade & Sourcing (Import/Export Logistics)
* **Focus:** Sourcing, Custom Clearing, Cargo & Freight Logistics.
* **Prompt:**
  > A realistic, high-end commercial photograph of a bustling deep-water shipping port at sunrise, 16:9 aspect ratio. On the right third of the frame, a professional logistics coordinator in a safety vest and hard hat holds a tablet, overlooking massive stacks of colorful shipping containers and a giant cargo crane. The left side of the image features a smooth, naturally blurred view of the ocean, calm water, and the distant silhouette of a cargo ship, creating clean negative space for text. Warm golden morning sunlight, professional architectural/industrial lifestyle photography, rich detail, high contrast, shallow depth of field, single unified image, --ar 16:9
